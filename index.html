Key AS-IS Insights from Dataset
🔋
Low Battery is the #1 Availability Problem
118 of 324 records (36.4%) have Low_Battery_Flag = 1. Vehicles with battery <20% before service have avg rating of only 3.47★ vs 4.09★ for 40–60% battery — a 0.62-star gap directly linked to KPI 3.
👻
Ghost Vehicles Inflate Response Times
59 records (18.2%) have Ghost_Vehicle_Flag = 1 (GPS_Drift >10m). Zones Z08, Z15, Z09 have the highest ghost counts. These directly inflate KPI 2 as technicians waste time searching.
📍
Zone Z12 Has Lowest FAR: 7.7%
Downtown zone Z12 has the lowest fleet availability rate (7.7%) — almost zero ready vehicles. This directly violates ADA equity requirements and contributes to the lowest ACR in the city.
📱
Manual Dispatch Slower than System
Manual dispatch (126 events) averages higher search times than System dispatch (198 events). WhatsApp-based coordination is the root cause — Mission Triggers would eliminate this gap.
⭐
ACR Gap: 0.37 Stars Below Target
Overall ACR = 3.83★ vs target 4.2★. Battery level, vehicle damage, and GPS drift are the three leading drivers of lower ratings. Fixing KPI 1 directly improves KPI 3.
🎯
Proposed Solution Impact (TO-BE)
Mission Triggers + high-precision IoT sensors projected to raise FAR to ≥95%, maintain ART ≤28 min, and push ACR to ≥4.2★ by eliminating ghost vehicles and automating low-battery response.
