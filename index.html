⚠ KPI 2 vs Full Response Chain — they are different things
KPI 2 (ART) = only the time technician takes to physically find and reach the vehicle (search step). Before: 18.1 min. After: ~14 min. Improves because GPS drift flagging cuts ghost vehicle search from 42 → 25 min.
Full Response Chain (130 min → 19 min) = all 5 steps combined: Detect battery (40 min saved) + Assign technician (25 min saved) + Search vehicle (KPI 2 step: ~4 min saved) + Service + Update. The 85% time reduction comes from automating detection and assignment, not from KPI 2 itself.

Conclusion — What all 6 graphs tell together

Every graph traces back to two root columns: Battery_Pct_Before_Service drives Graph 1 (FAR), Graph 2 (Downtime), Graph 4 (ACR by zone), and Graph 5 (Rating by battery). GPS_Drift_Meters drives Graph 3 (ART by zone) and Graph 6 (Response chain). Fix those two columns — batteries and GPS — and all six graphs improve across all three tabs.

Before and After dashboard have the same 6 graphs. Only the numbers change. The Before and After Analysis tab shows both datasets side by side on the same charts so the improvement is immediately visible.
